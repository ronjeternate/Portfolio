Memorix

Productivity App Dev Design

Memorix is an innovative learning tool designed for students and educators. It empowers users to create personalized questionnaires, flashcards, and notes while organizing them into classes. With features to seamlessly share resources with classmates, Memorix enhances collaboration and simplifies study sessions. Perfect for active learners aiming to streamline their educational experience!
